Speaking Engagements

Broadcaster, Public Speaker & Historical Consultant

Sinéad is an experienced public speaker and broadcaster. Over the years she has spoken in Ireland and internationally on Ireland, Irish history and art.

She is a cultural commentator and an expert on the history of Irish women from the 1880s to the 1980s. She welcomes opportunities to discuss this area of expertise and believes in understanding the past in order to negotiate in the present, and work towards a better future.

What I Can Offer

Areas of Expertise

Irish Women's History

As an expert on the history of Irish women from the 1880s to the 1980s, Sinéad offers engaging talks on this fascinating period of Irish history. She brings decades of research and scholarship to illuminate the lives and contributions of Irish women who shaped the nation.

Contact Sinéad to speak

Curatorial Practice & Public History

Sinéad lectures on curatorial practice, women and gender museums, and public history, with special focus on the Decade of Centenaries. Her insights draw from extensive experience in museum curation and historical interpretation.

Contact Sinéad to speak

Family History Research & Writing

Sinéad teaches techniques for writing and researching family history. Her decades of experience mean her talks focus on use of sources. She has a growing interest in exploring areas such as DNA and medical insights into genetics as a tool to discover our family history.

Contact Sinéad to speak
