Sinead McCoole

Irish Historian and Art Historian

Welcome to the world of Irish women's history. Sinéad McCoole is an award-winning historian, author, and curator dedicated to uncovering and sharing the remarkable stories of Irish women who shaped our past.

Through meticulously researched books, engaging exhibitions, and powerful storytelling across multiple media, Sinéad brings forgotten voices back to life and illuminates the crucial role women played in Ireland's history.

Explore her collection of critically acclaimed books, discover her theatrical works, and learn about the exhibitions that have captivated audiences across Ireland and beyond.
